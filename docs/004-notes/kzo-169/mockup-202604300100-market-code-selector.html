Transaction form — market_code selector & symbol disambiguation

Three states of the existing transaction-entry form after KZO-169. The chip selector at the top of the form scopes the autocomplete; in All mode the autocomplete shows disambiguated rows (e.g. BHP · AU vs BHP · US). Currency is derived from instrument.market_code and locked. Account dropdown filters to currency-compatible accounts; when none exist, an inline link launches KZO-179’s create-account flow pre-populated with the required currency.
